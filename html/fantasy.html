Why I Like Fantasy Football

Fantasy football is one of my favorite hobbies because it combines sports with strategy. I like tracking stats, matchups, and making weekly decisions based on data.

My Weekly Strategy

  1. Before Thursday Night Football
    • Check injury reports
    • Review matchups
    • Look at projected points
  2. Sunday Morning
    • Set final lineup
    • Check weather
  3. After Games
    • Review usage (targets, carries, snaps)
    • Plan waiver moves
